IPL 2020 TO BE HELD IN UAE. -BCCI

DUE TO MANY CORONA CASES IN INDIA IPL WAS SCHEDULED IN A COUNTRY WITH LESS COVID CASES ie AUSTRALIA OR UAE.BUT DUE TO BIG BASH LEAGUE AND AUSTRALIAN OPEN,IPL WAS TO BE HELD IN UAE INSTEAD OF AUSTRALIA.The three venues that have been put in place for the IPL 2020 season are Dubai International Cricket Stadium (Dubai), Sheikh Zayed Cricket Stadium (Abu Dhabi) and Sharjah Cricket Stadium (Sharjah). The distribution of matches isn't even in all three.

8 TEAMS WHICH WILL PLAY FOR IPL CUP 2020. - BCCI

Earlier it was said that may be 10 teams will be a part of IPL 2020 but the 2 other teams will be added in 2022 as per the meeting held by BCCI. the Teams will be as follows:

  • MI
  • DC
  • SRH

IPL 2020 POSTPONED TO SEPTEMBER 19,2020 -SOME PLAYER OF CSK CAUGHT CORONA

CSK players have caught corona during the practice and warm-up matches so IPL 2020 will be postponed to september 19 with opening matches held between CSK and MI following by matches of DC and KXIP.
